Armada Signs Justin Michael for “Too Late Tomorrow” featuring vocals from Matt Beilis!

Los Angeles DJ and producer Justin Michael, is a new addition to the Armada music roster releasing a collaboration with New York singer and songwriter Matt Beilis titled “Too Late Tomorrow“. The collaboration brings us an eclectic mix of progressive house, catchy beats, and soulful vocals making you fall in love with every moment.


After receiving a lot of attention through his YouTube videos and hype off his recent debut album, Justin Michael invited Matt Beilis to Los Angeles to lay down vocals for his fluently flowing and warm club-track “Too Late Tomorrow“. With Matt’s raspy vocals and blend of rock influence we get a uplifting vocal house track that’s perfect for both the clubs and festivals alike!
