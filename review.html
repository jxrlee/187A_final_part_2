Review: RE:Generation Music Project (2012) [Full Video]

The Re:Generation Music Project takes 5 different DJs and pairs them up with a different genre of music – rock, jazz, r&b, classical, and country. Who thought up of this genius idea? The Grammys…Watch and follow DJ Premier, Mark Ronson, Skrillex, Pretty Lights, and The Crystal Method as they all re-create and add their unique twist to traditional styles of music that our parents generation grew up listening to.


Following alongside these artists we are able to see the exploration of how music from the past has influenced today’s music. We get a taste of the classical perfection of the Berklee Symphony Orchestra to the bayou jams of New Orleans jazz, during which Amir Bar-Lev, the director of Re:Generation, reveals an artists creative process in generating the music using today’s technology to mix in the musical styles of past generations, more specifically in electronic dance music.
