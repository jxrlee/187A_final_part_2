Manufactured Superstars & Jeziel Quintela feat. Christian Burns – Silver Splits The Blue (Extended Mix)

Vocal house music is getting more predominate as electronic music keeps rising in popularity, and when done right, can be very beautiful when mixed into any dance track. Uncorking their latest jewel, “Silver Splits The Blue“, the Manufactured Superstars & Jeziel Quintela return to the party capitol of the world featuring mighty Christian Burns on vocal duty.


The music video for “Silver Splits The Blue” was shot at XS Nightclub in Las Vegas where the Manufactured Superstars are going on their second year as resident DJs. The part owners of Beatport have already acomplished more than anyone can imagine in their music career, the spacemen have created the perfect track for the carefree as summer begins. Closing a recording deal with Black Hole Recordings only insures that in 2012 the duo will rise to the forefront of the EDM scene. Take a listen to Silver Splits The Blue and watch the official music video below!
