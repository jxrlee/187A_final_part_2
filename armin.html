A State of Trance 550 – Livesets, Track Listings, and Downloads

The world tour of Armin van Buuren’s ASOT 550 (A State of Trance) officially kicked off this month on March 1st! The global radio show opened up the party at the renowned Ministry of Sound club in London, UK which sold out within hours of releasing tickets. Armin van Buuren is making a world tour, stopping in 5 different countries consisting of 6 stops in the cities of London, Moscow, Kiev, Los Angeles, Miami, and Den Bosch.


Each event will be broadcasted live on www.astateoftrance.com which on average brings in a total of 25 million listeners every week. However, the amount of listeners is expected to be much higher for the milestone event.


If you are tuning in any of the broadcasts make sure you use the #asot550 hashtag and @ASOTlive on Twitter or by replying to the posts on the A State of Trance, Armin, or Armada Music Facebook pages! ASOT 550 marks another milestone the trance community and electronic dance music alike.
